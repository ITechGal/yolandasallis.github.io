Live Chat Support Scenario

Example of how I greet customers, ask clarifying questions, and document solutions in a friendly tone.

Customer: Hi, I can’t log into my account.

Yolanda: Hi! I’m happy to help. Are you seeing an error message when you try to sign in?

Customer: Yes, it says “account not found.”

Yolanda: Thank you. I’ll first confirm the email on your account. Can you please verify the email you’re using to sign in?

…conversation continues through verification, password reset link, and final confirmation…
