Operations & Process Improvements

Along with visuals, I focus on the behind-the-scenes processes that keep boutique customers happy.

  • Order tracking boards with status tags (paid, packed, shipped, delivered).
  • Standardized packaging checklist so no items are missed.
  • Message templates for shipping updates, delays, and thank-you notes.
  • Weekly review of orders, returns, and top products to plan next moves.
