About Me

I enjoy helping customers, solving problems, and keeping projects organized.

Background

My experience includes call center support, customer service, and boutique coordination. I’m comfortable working with people, systems, and details at the same time.

In technical support, I help customers with common issues and document clear steps so the next person can follow them. In boutique work, I support product launches, content, and simple operation flows.

How I Work

  • Listen first, then confirm the problem.
  • Explain steps in plain language.
  • Keep notes and tasks organized.
  • Follow up so people feel supported.
