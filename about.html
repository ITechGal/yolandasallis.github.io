About Me

Technical Support Specialist and Boutique Project Coordinator who enjoys solving problems, helping customers, and creating organized systems.

My Background

I have experience across customer service, call-center support, boutique brand coordination, and technical troubleshooting. I understand both the customer-facing experience and the behind-the-scenes operations that make a business run smoothly.

On the technical side, I assist customers with login errors, device setup, software issues, and general troubleshooting. I'm comfortable working within ticketing systems and documenting solutions clearly so customers can follow along with confidence.

On the boutique side, I coordinate collection launches, product descriptions, images, branding assets, and content calendars. I enjoy combining creativity with organization, making sure every project has structure and flow.

What I’m Good At

  • Breaking down technical problems in simple steps
  • Providing patient and friendly customer support
  • Managing multiple tasks with organization
  • Creating easy-to-follow tools, templates, and workflows

How I Work

Customer-First Mindset

I focus on listening, confirming the issue, and explaining solutions clearly. My goal is for every customer to feel supported and understood.

Organized & Reliable

I like turning chaos into clarity — using trackers, templates, and timelines to keep projects running smoothly from start to finish.
