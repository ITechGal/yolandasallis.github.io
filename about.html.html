About Me

Technical Support Specialist and Boutique Project Coordinator who enjoys solving problems and creating organized systems.

My Background

My experience spans customer service, call center support, and boutique project coordination. I have worked in fast-paced environments where clear communication, patience, and follow-through are critical.

On the technical side, I support customers with login issues, device setup, software questions, and general troubleshooting. I am comfortable using ticketing tools, documenting steps, and guiding non-technical users through solutions.

On the boutique side, I coordinate product launches, manage content calendars, and support branding and visual design. I enjoy connecting the front-end customer experience with the behind-the-scenes work that makes everything run smoothly.

What I’m Good At

  • Breaking down technical issues in plain language.
  • Keeping track of details across multiple tasks.
  • Following up so customers feel taken care of.
  • Creating simple tools (checklists, templates, docs) that teams can reuse.

How I Work

Customer-First Mindset

Whether I am on a call, chat, or email, I focus on listening first, confirming the issue, and explaining the next steps clearly. I aim to make customers feel supported, not rushed.

Organized & Reliable

I like turning chaos into a simple plan—organizing tickets, launch tasks, or content schedules so everyone knows what is happening and when.
